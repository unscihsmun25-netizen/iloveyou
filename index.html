i don't want to love you,

i want to learn you,

i want to know if you like your tea sweet or regular,

i want to know what your favorite flower is,

i want to know why you cried when you were 8 years old,

i want to know why you hate your home,

i want to reach the deep end of your heart,

i want to see all your sides,

the pretty and the dark,

and love them.

I want to see all your previous versions,

and love them.
